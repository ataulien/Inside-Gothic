    Perceptions

    Whenever a Character notices (assesses) something in the world around it, a Perception is triggered. For each perception, a script-state can be registered to be started once that perception fires.

    Depending on the script-state entered upon assessing something, perceptions may be set up different, ie. Npcs reactions differ depending on their situations.

    Perceptions can be manually triggered from script-code (marked ) or are sent from the engine on certain actions (marked ).

    Active Perceptions

    Here is a list of all perceptions checked by each character individually every frame:

    • Assess Player:
      Triggered when the Player is nearby.

    • Assess Enemy:
      Triggered when a hostile Character is nearby.

    • Assess Fighter:
      Triggered when a nearby Character has their weapon out.
      Sent when someone transformed into a Bloodfly. (?)

    • Assess Body:
      Triggered when a nearby Character is dead or unconscious.

    • Assess Item:
      Triggered when an item is nearby.

    Note

    If multiple nearby Characters apply, the perception is only triggered for the closest one.

    Passive Perceptions

    Passive perceptions are usually broadcast to all nearby applicable Characters within a certain range.

    Note

    Perception ranges can be adjusted separately from the Daedalus-scripts.

    Fighting:

    • Assess Murder:
      Broadcast to nearby NPCs when someones/somethings Death was caused by another Character (Human or Monster).

    • Assess Defeat:
      Broadcast when a Character drops unconscious.

    • Assess Damage:
      Sent to the damaged NPC after receiving damage and when an attack was parried (reports 0 damage).

    • Assess Others damage:
      Always broadcast to nearby characters when an Assess Damage happened. -

    • Assess Threat:
      Sent to the NPC targeted with a ranged weapon.

    • Assess Draw Weapon:
      Broadcast when the player drew their weapon (ONLY the player!).

    • Assess Remove weapon:
      Broadcast when someone un-drew their weapon.

    • Assess Warn:
      Broadcast if someone saw pickpocketing.
      Broadcast if someone (weaker NPCs) is calling for guards (ie. if the player entered a forbidden room).
      Broadcast if someone witnessed stealing an item
      Broadcast if someone witnessed a sneaking Character (After "Observe Suspect")
      Broadcast if a character is asking their comrades for help
      Broadcast while monsters are warning the player (or any character) before they attack
      Broadcast if an Orc has assessed damage
      Broadcast if an Orc witnessed an intruder (After "Observe Intruder")

    Trickery:

    • Assess Enter Room:
      Broadcast when the Character has changed rooms (went outside → inside, inside → outside or changed rooms within the same building)
      Broadcast after sleeping in a bed, so that the NPCs will notice the player being in the room again instead of letting the player steal everything.

    • Observe Intruder:
      Broadcast when the Player stopped moving and is not sneaking. (?)

    • Catch Thief:
      Broadcast after pickpocketing when the victim is aware of the action.

    • Assess Theft:
      Broadcast when an item was picked up. Script figures out if taking the item really was theft.
      Broadcast to nearby Characters during pickpocketing. (There better be nobody nearby...)

    • Assess Fake Guild:
      Broadcast to nearby characters when someone transformed into another creature, making them remember that you tried to fake your guild. (Better not have anyone else nearby when transforming!)

    • Observe Suspect:
      Broadcast when a Character is sneaking. Usually results in nearby NPCs asking "Why are you sneaking?".

    • Assess Surprise:
      Broadcast when someone transformed back from being a monster.

    • Assess Use Mob:
      Broadcast when a Character starts using an interactive object. (ie. fiddling with a locked chest)

    Sound:

    • Assess Quiet Sound:
      Broadcast every time a footstep-sound is played. Also broadcast when two (physics) objects collided (ie. arrows hitting the ground).

    • Assess Fight Sound:
      Broadcast when a Character got hit during a fight.
      Broadcast when someone got hit by magic (Assess Magic).

    Talking:

    • Assess Call:
      Sent to the NPC the player (or an other NPC) wants to talk to, if it is not
      within talking range. Usually plays a "Come over here" animation and waits
      until the player comes closer.

    • Assess Talk:
      Sent to the NPC the player (or an other NPC) wants to talk to, if it is within
      talking range, ie. after pressing CTRL+Up in front of an NPC. Usually begins a dialogue between the player and the NPC.

    • Npc Command:
      Used for finding a smalltalk parter.
      Sent when an NPC wants their item back ("Give it back to me!", not sure whether that is even used, actually)

    Items:

    • Assess Given Item:
      (Seems to be unused) Sent to the receiving NPC during an item-trade (ie. when shopping)

    World:

    • Move Mob:
      Broadcast when a door is blocking the path of an AI controlled character.

    • Move Npc:
      Broadcast when an other Character is blocking the path of an AI controlled character.

    Magic:

    • Assess Magic:
      Sent to NPCs hit by magic effects, has access to spell type and category.

    • Assess Stopmagic:
      Sent to NPCs which got hit by magic effects (Assess Magic) when the magic effect stopped (tied to the actual particle effect).

    • Assess Caster:
      Broadcast when someone starts investing mana in a magic spell.
